What is Codeforce?

  • A recurring meetup for people building with and curious about Generative AI
  • Short talks, live demos, and open discussion — no slides-only lectures
  • Open to all levels: from "what's a prompt?" to "I fine-tuned my own model"

Why we're here

GenAI is moving fast — new models, tools and techniques every week.

Codeforce is a space to share what's working, learn from each other, and build community around it.
